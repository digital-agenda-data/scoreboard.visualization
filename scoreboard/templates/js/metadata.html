Definitions and scopes:

EC effective cofinancing rate for SMEs partner in FP7-ICT projects

  • Definition and scope: The cofinancing rate is the % of projects total costs which are covered by EC funding grants. Figures include all partners and third parties (as subcontractors). SMEs are defined as Small and medium individual private enterprises with less than 250 employed persons and a turnover less than or equal 50 millions euro or a balance sheet total less than or equal 43 millions euro.
